// The Multi-Cloud Advantage

Why Go Multi-Cloud?

🔓

Avoid Vendor Lock-In

Stay flexible and negotiate from a position of strength. A multi-cloud strategy prevents dependency on a single provider and ensures portability.

⚡

Best-of-Breed Services

Pick the strongest capabilities from each platform. Use AWS Lambda for serverless, Azure for enterprise identity, and GCP for data analytics.

🌐

Geographic Redundancy

Distribute workloads across multiple providers and regions for maximum resilience, lower latency, and compliance with local data residency laws.

📈

Cost Optimisation

Compare pricing across providers in real time. Shift workloads to the most cost-effective platform and leverage spot instances, savings plans, and reserved capacity.
