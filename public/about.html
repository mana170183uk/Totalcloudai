// Our Story

How We Got Here

TotalCloudAI was founded with a singular mission: to democratise enterprise-grade cloud services and make them accessible to businesses of every size.

Based at Maxi House, Halesfield 20, Telford TF7 4QU, we are a UK-based cloud consultancy that bridges the gap between complex cloud technologies and real business outcomes. Our team of certified cloud architects, DevOps engineers, and AI specialists brings deep expertise across Azure, AWS, GCP, and Alibaba Cloud.

From cloud infrastructure design and migration to DevOps automation and AI-powered solutions, we partner with organisations to reduce costs, accelerate innovation, and build resilient digital foundations. Every engagement is tailored, every solution is built with security by design, and every result is measurable.

Registered in England & Wales as TotalCloudAI Limited, we are committed to transparent partnerships, honest advice, and delivering outcomes that matter.
